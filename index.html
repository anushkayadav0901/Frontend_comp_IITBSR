Student Clubs & Activities

Join a variety of student clubs, from coding and robotics to music and drama. Hostel life is full of opportunities to explore your interests and make lifelong friends.

Hostel Events

Participate in annual hostel fests, cultural nights, and inter-hostel competitions. Every event is a celebration of diversity and talent.

Study Spaces

Quiet study rooms and group discussion areas are available 24/7, ensuring you have the perfect environment for academic success.

Sports & Fitness

Enjoy access to sports complexes, gymnasiums, and open grounds. Whether you play cricket, football, badminton, or just want to jog, there's something for everyone.

Mess & Food

Relish a variety of cuisines in the hostel mess, with special menus for festivals and health-conscious options for fitness enthusiasts.

Nightlife & Community

Late-night study sessions, group discussions, and midnight snacks foster a strong sense of community and belonging among hostel residents.
